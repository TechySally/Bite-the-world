Frequently Asked Questions

Event Basics

What is Bite the World?
Bite the World is a 3-day food and culture event where guests explore global cuisines, attend cooking demos, and enjoy performances from around the world.

When and where is the event?
It takes place on August 13-15 in Anaheim California.

Is the event open to the public?
Yes! Students, locals, and visitors are all welcome.

Tickets & Registration

How do I buy tickets?
Click the “Buy Tickets” button on our homepage or visit the ticket page. You can also use the link in our Instagram bio!

Are tickets refundable?
Unfortunately, all ticket sales are final. However, you can transfer your ticket to someone else if needed.

Other Questions

Will there be vegetarian or allergy-friendly options?
Yes! Many of our vendors offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes. Please check with individual booths for allergy info.

How can I volunteer?
Visit our Contact Us page and send us a message with “Volunteer” in the subject line.
